What to Pack

Vail sits at 8,150 ft ‚Äî the altitude, sun, and cold are no joke. Here's what to bring.

‚õ∑ Ski & Snow Gear

  • Ski jacket & snow pants (waterproof)
  • Base layers (moisture-wicking, not cotton)
  • Ski socks (wool or synthetic ‚Äî bring extras)
  • Helmet (or rent on-mountain)
  • Goggles & sunglasses
  • Gloves or mittens + thin liner gloves
  • Neck gaiter / balaclava
  • Hand & toe warmers

üß• Everyday Layers

  • Warm mid-layer (fleece or down jacket)
  • Casual warm clothes for the house
  • Cozy loungewear for hot tub nights
  • Waterproof boots with traction
  • Warm hat & beanie
  • Swimsuit (hot tub!)
  • One nice-ish outfit for birthday dinner

üèî Altitude Essentials

  • Sunscreen SPF 50+ ‚Äî UV is 60% stronger at altitude
  • Lip balm with SPF
  • Water bottle ‚Äî drink twice what you normally do
  • Ibuprofen / Advil ‚Äî for altitude headaches
  • Moisturizer ‚Äî the air is very dry
  • Electrolyte packets (Liquid IV, Nuun, etc.)
  • Avoid alcohol your first night ‚Äî it hits different up here

üéí Don't Forget

  • Phone charger & portable battery
  • Camera for mountain photos
  • Reusable grocery bags (for group grocery runs)
  • Card/board games for house nights
  • Any prescriptions or medications
  • Snacks for the road if driving from Denver
  • A birthday gift or card for Trish!
